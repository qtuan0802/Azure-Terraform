Naming Convention Explanation
Format: sapsfbatch-sit-<category>-<name>

Benefits: - ✅ Shorter and more readable (avg. 30 chars vs 50+ chars) - ✅ Follows Azure best practices - ✅ Groups secrets by category (sapsf, auth-basic, auth-oauth) - ✅ Easy to filter and search in Azure Portal - ✅ Consistent with Azure resource naming patterns - ✅ No redundancy (sapsf not repeated)
